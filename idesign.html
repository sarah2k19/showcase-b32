About the project

iDesign is an educational platform where children can execute projects following a DBL approach. The projects are based on different primary school subjects. In a project space guidance is offered through tips and hints. We offer a place where children can develop attitude, skills, knowledge and talents in their own pace. For these children there is also guidance through tips and hints. The content of these tips is made to suit the level of the student, and the type of guidance required. Next to this, to support the learning process of the children, we offer a place for teachers to broaden their teaching methods. In addition to this, IDesign is also a starting point for schools to explore new teaching methods safely, to benefit the development of young students. As a result they will be better prepared for the future where they will have to work in multidisciplinary teams.

Reflection

This semester I developed my business side, and I applied past knowledge and experience with preparing user tests, prototyping methods, and teamwork. On top of this I learned a crucial lesson in communication. Having focused on the business side, I learned the value of implementing the business side. Before starting this project I thought that the business side was all about making as much money as possible. This semester I found out that the business side is actually all about finding ways to create value for as much people as possible. It is also about analyzing how to best reach your customers, and to find the best way to offer your value, and how to make money off of it to keep your company healthy and keep reaching more people/transforming more lives. I used this knowledge to make a Business Model Canvas and a Value Proposition.
