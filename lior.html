About the project

Lior is a informative decorative phone case, with coloured tiles on the case. The phone case was designed to improve the experience of user-notification, and the case is linked to whatsapp. Whatsapp groups can be tagged with a certain category, like ‘Friends’ ‘Family’ or ‘Groupwork’. Each group is then linked to a colour. When a message is received from this text group, a tile on the phone case will light up in this colour. The target group for Lior are students and young professionals. The design of the case is a result of several user interviews. The final design was evaluated with two students. The outcome showed that our phone case could be used to decrease the amount of unnecessary unlocking phones to check for messages. It could also possibly improve the experience of formal and informal group meetings. Future improvements include improving the distribution of light in the case.
